The definition of POWER
from the POV of Vergil
In the shadows of the demonic realm, power pulsates like a dark heartbeat. To me, Vergil, power is not a mere force but a reflection of one's essencethe embodiment of an individual's true self. In the cold steel of Yamato, I find not just a weapon but a conduit to the depths of my own being. Power is not given; it is taken, seized with relentless determination. It is the key to unlocking the dormant potential within, a force that courses through my veins, defining the very core of my existence. Through mastery of self, I transcend the limitations of the mundane and ascend to a realm where my every desire becomes a tangible reality. True strength emanates from within, a fusion of mind, body, and soul. It is the unwavering resolve to confront the shadows that dance within, to embrace the darkness and mold it into a formidable force. Yamato, my loyal companion, is not just a blade but an extension of my essencea testament to the power that lies dormant within each of us. Yet, with power comes a delicate dance on the precipice of corruption. The allure of greater strength can tempt even the noblest of intentions, leading to a struggle between the light and the shadows within. It is a journey fraught with internal conflicts and moral quandaries, a path where the pursuit of perfection treads dangerously close to the edge. In my pursuit of power, I seek not only dominance over my foes but mastery over myself. Each battle, each conquest, is a step towards a higher plane of existencea relentless climb towards the summit of my own potential. The pursuit of power is not a means to an end; it is a journey, a relentless odyssey towards a perfected version of myself. In the tapestry of the demonic cosmos, power is the thread that weaves through the fabric of existence. It is the heartbeat of the warrior, the resonance of the soul, and the catalyst for transcendence. As Vergil, I stand at the nexus of power and destiny, forging my own path through the chaos, seeking the pinnacle of strength and the perfection that lies beyond.
